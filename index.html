Why Allegory?

Because insurance is not about punishment or discrimination but preventing and protecting. That’s why we never use your age, gender, credit score, where you live, or how many accidents you have had in the past to calculate your insurance premium. Our mission is clear: "Let’s rewrite the “allegory” of individualized insurance together!”

Automatic Tracking

Pair your phone with your in car bluetooth, or just let the app detect when you’ve started driving. We will automatically record your trips.

Accurate Driving Scores

Allegory insurance premiums are based on the scores you see. We don’t just believe we’re providing accurate scores, we’re betting on it!

Personal Insurance

We believe insurance premium should be based on the choices you make. You’re not rated on your accident history, or who you are, only the choices you make.

Theft Prevention

When you’re insured with Allegory you’ll be sent a blackbox device. In case of theft this device will find your vehicle.

Stay Safe From Fraud

All Allegory drivers are equipped with dash cams keeping our drivers safe from fraudulent third party claims.
